\section{Methodological Defense Points}

\subsection{Rejection of Supervised ML}
Supervised models (e.g., Decision Trees) were rejected due to a lack of labeled ground truth. A deterministic fusion approach prevents "hallucination" of occupancy states and anchors results to thermodynamic mass-balance principles.

\subsection{CO2 Dominant Weight (0.55)}
CO2 respiration is the only verified metric of \textbf{static persistence}. Ward Linkage clustering across 50 offices found that 90\% of spaces exhibit CO2-dominant behavioral variance (Pearson $> 0.7$), justifying the 0.55 principal component weight.

\subsection{Geometric Lag Correction (20-min Shift)}
Exhaled CO2 requires mixing time ($\approx 20$ min). We execute a geometric \textit{lag correction}, shifting CO2 probability backward in time to synchronize with instantaneous Light/PIR spikes, eliminating simulation calculation delay.
