\section*{Methodological Defense Points (Sections 1--5)}

\subsection{1. Rejection of Supervised Machine Learning}
Deterministic fusion avoids the "hallucination" of occupancy states in the absence of ground-truth labels. By anchoring predictions to verified thermodynamic mass-balance (CO$_2$), we ensure physical reproducibility.

\subsection{2. Empirical Validation of 0.55 CO$_2$ Weight}
Hierarchical Ward Linkage clustering across 50 offices revealed that \textbf{90\% of spaces exhibit highly-correlated CO$_2$ variance (Pearson $> 0.7$)}, validating CO$_2$ as the building-wide principal component for persistence.

\subsection{3. Geometric Lag Correction (22-min Shift)}
Exhaled CO$_2$ gas transport delay is neutralized by shifting probability streams backward by $t-22$ minutes, synchronizing metabolic triggers with instantaneous user actions.

\subsection{4. Exclusion of Thermodynamic Sensors}
Temperature and Humidity signals are discarded due to HVAC automation noise. CO$_2$ remains the singular reliable proxy governed by metabolic mass-conservation.

\subsection{5. Concept Definition: Sensor Fusion}
Merging independent modalities (CO$_2$, Light, PIR) cancels individual error states (e.g., PIR stillness blinds), a benchmark derived from \textbf{Sun et al. (2020)}.

\section{Section 9: Micro-Analysis of Behavioral Logic}
The algorithm manages four distinct temporal phases:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Arrival (Light Leads):} Triggering occupancy 20--30m before CO$_2$ accumulation.
    \item \textbf{Persistence (CO$_2$ Role):} Sustaining occupied state during PIR quiet periods.
    \item \textbf{PIR Confirmation:} Reinforcement of active state confidence.
    \item \textbf{Departure:} Score drop below 0.35 upon deactivation.
\end{enumerate}
